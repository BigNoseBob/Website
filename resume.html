Undergraduate Research at the MIT Space Propulsion Lab

STEP-1 Satellite

Lead by phD candidate Gustav Pettersson, the STEP-1 project serves as a demonstration of the research performed by Dr. Oliver Jia-Richards on staged electrospray propulsion systems. In order to achieve this, MIT's Space Propulsion Lab (SPL) is designing and manufacturing a 3U cubesat which will house the staged electrospray propulsion system alongside various other scientific equipment.
STEP-1 has a number of undergraduate researchers (UROPs) working on the project split into subteams. STEP-1 is outfitted with a camera subsystem, of which, most of the UROPs reside on. I reside on the electronics team of the camera payload subsystem. Working alongside two other UROPs, we create and design electrical schematics and PCBs using KiCAD.
